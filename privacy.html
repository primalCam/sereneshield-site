Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-17

What we do

SereneShield filters content client-side using keyword logic in your browser. We don’t need your social media credentials and we don’t track your browsing.

Data we collect

  • Payment & receipts: When you purchase Pro via Stripe, Stripe processes your payment. We receive minimal info necessary for receipts/support (e.g., email, purchase status).
  • Local settings: Your preferences (e.g., custom keywords, counters) are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.
  • Support: If you email us, we receive the info you send.

What we don’t collect

  • No tracking pixels.
  • No personal feed content sent to our servers.
  • No third-party analytics on the extension.

Third parties

Payments are handled by Stripe. Their use of data is governed by their own policy.

Data retention

Payment records are retained for accounting/chargeback purposes. Local extension settings remain on your device until you clear them.

Your rights

You can request deletion of support records we control. For payment data, contact Stripe or us and we’ll help route the request.
